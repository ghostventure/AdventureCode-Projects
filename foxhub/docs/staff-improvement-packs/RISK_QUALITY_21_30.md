# Risk and Quality Staff Improvements 21-30

This slice defines staff-side controls for account security, service timing, routing, quality review, privacy handling, policy traceability, incident review, and abuse detection.

## Improvements

21. Staff Account Security Panel: tracks MFA, device sessions, last login, role age, inactive accounts, and suspicious staff access.
22. SLA Timers: adds response and resolution timers for support, fraud, appeals, payments, privacy, and safety queues.
23. Auto-Prioritization: ranks cases by harm, payment risk, legal exposure, safety severity, age, and repeat reports.
24. User Communication Templates: gives staff approved case messages for holds, approvals, denials, appeals, evidence requests, and recoveries.
25. Escalation Tree: routes support work to trust, fraud, security, payments, legal, privacy, or managers with clear ownership.
26. Quality Assurance Sampling: samples closed cases for manager review, consistency checks, coaching, and policy gaps.
27. Staff Notes Privacy Levels: separates notes into internal, manager-only, legal-only, and user-visible summaries.
28. Policy Versioning: attaches the active policy version and public reason to staff decisions.
29. Incident Postmortems: records timeline, root cause, impact, staff actions, and follow-up tasks after major incidents.
30. Abuse Pattern Detection: identifies coordinated reporting, scam rings, duplicate devices, linked accounts, and repeated abuse patterns.
