CLTCH.NTWRK is a live-talent marketplace connecting hosts, venues, musicians, and DJs through a fast booking pipeline. This page is the public reference point for media requests, founder background, and product positioning.

Company Snapshot

  • Brand: CLTCH.NTWRK
  • Category: Live entertainment booking marketplace
  • Primary users: Hosts, venues, musicians, and DJs
  • Core product: Real-time local matching for live performance opportunities

What CLTCH.NTWRK Does

The platform is built to reduce booking friction. Hosts can post gig needs quickly, and performers can surface and respond based on fit, availability, distance, and payout readiness. The goal is to make same-day and near-term live bookings more reliable.
