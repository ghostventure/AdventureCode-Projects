Fast answers for new starters

The next six cards share proactive guidance most users need before asking a question.

Account creation

Verify email first, then upload government ID and a selfie (18+). Performance or host-only accounts take 5 minutes.

Document safety

Papers like IDs never reach CLTCH servers; we rely on vetted verification partners, and you can delete them anytime.

Booking fees

Hosts cover a 2% processing fee; performers charge their own rate. Change settings in Billing > Pricing.

Payout workflow

Payouts hit the linked bank, Apple Pay, or Google Pay after each booking settles; you see fees and net in the dashboard.

Local compliance

City, county, parish, and borough obligations are mapped automatically. Manual overrides let you highlight special permits.

Reporting issues

Flag safety, payments, or child-labor concerns through legal@cltch.ntwrk; we respond in 24 hours.

Marketing & visibility

Complete your performer page, add tags, and respond to inquiries within three hours to stay featured on the Gig Radar.

Host checklists

Upload venue insurance, safety plans, and child safety protocols before posting gigs to unlock premium exposure.

Guided onboarding

We send an onboarding walkthrough to every new account that covers compliance, deposits, and talent scouting tools.

Safety first

Every contract includes easy emergency contacts, insurance reminders, and optional check-in requirements.
