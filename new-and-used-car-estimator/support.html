Decision Support

Signals to review before you trust the number

Risk review

Condition, title history, accident history, rust, records, and mileage can overpower the baseline.

Negotiation lane

Use the low end of the range against written quotes, asking prices, and inspection findings.

Documentation

Confirm title, service records, recalls, tire/brake condition, equipment, and seller disclosures.

Category scope

Make/model/category selections are controlled so unsupported vehicles are not guessed.

Mass-market rule

In this estimator, Mass-Market generally requires at least five active mainstream auto model lines plus broad everyday-buyer coverage. Narrow, paused, discontinued, motorcycle-only, specialty, or underrepresented brands are classified under Niche, Mid-Market, Rugged, Sport, Motorcycle, or International instead.

SUV-only lane

The SUVs only category filters by SUV body-style segments, not brand label, so sedans, pickups, vans, motorcycles, and box trucks stay out of that view.

Crossover lane

The Crossovers category uses SUV-style segments but excludes rugged, truck-based, minivan, and commercial-van names so common crossover models stay grouped separately.

Comparable checklist

Compare price, mileage, distance, title, accident history, seller type, and days listed.

Confidence explainer

Quality changes with mileage, condition, title history, market pressure, and profile specificity.

Operations

Resilience and guardrails

Catalog integrity

Duplicate IDs, missing segments, category gaps, and year-bound conflicts are smoke checked.

Input guard

Range controls are clamped and invalid selects recover to the nearest controlled option.

Tamper status

The client computes a catalog signature and freezes catalog arrays during runtime.

Fail-safe mode

If a guarded audit fails, the estimator renders a conservative fallback instead of breaking.

Snapshot state

The latest safe estimate is stored locally for recovery awareness.

Smoke status

Browser smoke verifies pricing controls, catalog filters, support components, and recovery paths.

Commercial And Motorcycle Support

Specialty vehicle context

Box trucks

Commercial includes cargo vans, cutaways, low-cab-forward trucks, medium-duty box trucks, liftgate-ready chassis, and fleet work platforms.

Commercial upfits

Box length, liftgate, refrigeration, shelving, payload rating, wheelbase, and duty cycle can change value more than cosmetic condition.

Motorcycles

Motorcycle profiles cover cruiser, sport, touring, adventure, dual-sport, scooter/mini, electric, and off-road categories.

Motorcycle checks

Review tire date codes, chain/belt/shaft service, brake wear, fork seals, service records, title status, modifications, track use, drops, and storage history.

Homework

What to verify before you use the number

Vehicle facts that move value

Year, make, model, trim, equipment, mileage, condition, title status, accident history, records, rust, and region can all move the benchmark.

Costs outside the benchmark

Taxes, registration, title, documentation fees, dealer add-ons, delivery, warranties, APR, transport, storage, and loan term are outside the benchmark.

Evidence to collect

Use current listings, written quotes, recall checks, service records, comparable sales, and independent inspections before buying.
