Methodology

The estimator uses static baselines plus transparent client-side adjustments. It is not a live appraisal feed.

Controls are keyboard-friendly native inputs, theme-aware, and readable in day or night mode. Commercial van and collector guidance should be used as a checklist, not a replacement for qualified inspection.

Inspection Reminders

Check recalls, tires, brakes, leaks, rust, battery health, title, emissions, records, and inspection findings.

For commercial vans, review shelves, racks, refrigeration, partitions, ladder racks, wrap removal, idle hours, and upfit wiring. For collector vehicles, review matching numbers, paint, interior, trim, drivetrain, provenance, and restoration records.

FAQ

Is this an appraisal?

No. This is a planning benchmark for comparison. It is not an offer, appraisal, lender value, insurance value, tax value, wholesale bid, or guaranteed sale price.

Where do the values come from?

The estimator uses static benchmark baselines and client-side adjustments. Users should compare the result against current listings, condition, options, and written out-the-door quotes.

Why is a vehicle missing?

Unsupported makes and models are intentionally not guessed. The controlled catalog can be expanded as better baseline data becomes available.

What does Market price mode do?

It lets users compare Soft market, Normal, and Inflation conditions. The current note is based on May 2026 CPI-U, new/used vehicle CPI movement, KBB/Cox average transaction prices, Manheim used-wholesale movement, and used-market residual-value context.

Privacy

This static estimator does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information through a form. Browser features such as local theme preference may be stored on the user's device.

Hosting providers may process routine technical data such as IP address, user agent, request time, and security logs. The data-minimization standard for this site is simple: do not ask for, store, or retain personal information unless there is a legitimate operational need.

Do not enter personal, financial, title, account, or sensitive information into this estimator.
