# Legal Sign-Off Workflow (2026-04-11)

Owner: EstateHat Legal / Compliance  
Applies to: Terms, Privacy, FAQ & Scope, Help Center, Assistant compliance guidance

## Purpose

Establish documented legal review and approval before publishing policy or liability-language changes.

## Required Sign-Off Stages

1. Draft complete
- Product owner links exact changed files/sections.
- Change summary includes jurisdiction impact (federal/state/county/territory).

2. Counsel review
- Licensed counsel reviews for enforceability, disclosures, and jurisdiction risks.
- Counsel marks one of: `Approved`, `Approved with required edits`, `Rejected`.

3. Required edits
- Product implements required edits.
- Counsel confirms final text.

4. Release approval
- Compliance lead confirms counsel approval is attached.
- Only then deploy legal-language changes.

## Evidence Requirements

- Counsel approval memo or email PDF in legal record storage.
- Redline/diff snapshot of final approved text.
- Effective date logged in release notes and on-page last-updated markers.

## Blocking Rule

If counsel approval artifact is missing, release is blocked for legal-copy changes.
