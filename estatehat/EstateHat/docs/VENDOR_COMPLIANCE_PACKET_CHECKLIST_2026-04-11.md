# Vendor Compliance Packet Checklist (2026-04-11)

Owner: Legal + Procurement + Security  
Scope: Escrow/title/payment/identity/background/infrastructure vendors

## Required Contractual Artifacts

- Executed master services agreement (MSA)
- Data processing addendum (DPA) where personal data is processed
- Security exhibit (minimum controls and incident notification obligations)
- Subprocessor disclosure (if applicable)
- SLA / uptime and support terms

## Required Security/Compliance Evidence

- SOC 2 Type II or equivalent attestation (or compensating evidence)
- Pen test or independent security assessment summary
- Incident response and breach notification process
- Data retention/deletion capabilities
- Encryption controls (in transit + at rest)

## Financial/Operational Boundary Confirmations

- Provider confirms funds custody role (if any)
- Provider confirms settlement/disbursement responsibilities
- Provider confirms licensing status relevant to service scope

## Review Cadence

- Initial onboarding review before activation
- Annual refresh of packet artifacts
- Immediate reassessment after material incident or control failure
