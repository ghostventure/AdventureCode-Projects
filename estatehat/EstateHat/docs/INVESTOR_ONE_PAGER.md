## What EstateHat does

EstateHat provides a trust-first software platform for peer-to-peer real estate transaction workflows, including profile readiness, document coordination, checklist-based progression, and in-platform messaging.

## Why now

- Transaction workflows remain fragmented for non-expert users.
- Trust and process clarity are major barriers to transaction conversion.
- Productized verification and guided UX can reduce drop-off and fraud risk.
