EstateHat Investor Brochure

Real estate workflow, trust, and transaction coordination in one platform.

EstateHat is a Raleigh-based software company building a trust-first operating layer for peer-to-peer real estate transactions. The product brings listing discovery, identity readiness, messaging, forms, and closing coordination into one workflow.

What EstateHat does

EstateHat is built for users who need a guided transaction flow instead of a patchwork of disconnected tools. The platform is structured around verified identities, role-aware controls, and clearer progression from discovery through closing readiness.

Current live surfaces include authenticated onboarding, Hat Board status views, listings and watchlists, transaction and messaging workflows, legal and FAQ pages, investor materials, and public company information routes.

What matters to investors

  • EstateHat is pursuing a software-margin model tied to transaction activity rather than direct principal exposure.

  • Near-term value creation depends on deeper integrations, stronger reporting cadence, and disciplined funnel conversion.

Trust and governance signals

  • Identity and role controls are part of the product narrative, not an afterthought.
  • Legal, privacy, accessibility, FAQ, press, and investor pages are publicly available.
  • Capital is being framed around product credibility, compliance hardening, and measured scaling instead of hype-led growth.

Current monetization snapshot

  • 1.50% platform fee on completed transaction sale price.
  • Buyer-paid by default unless the seller elects to absorb the fee.
  • Verified User subscription shown in product at $20 per month.
  • Escrow and wire amounts are modeled in-product but may be partner pass-through depending on transaction setup.

Near-term focus

  • Harden compliance and operational readiness for broader fundraising.
  • Optimize trust and transaction funnel conversion.
